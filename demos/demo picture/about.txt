## Inspiration
In this Covid times , there's a lockdown everywhere and the school classes are shifted to online mode,
through this webapp teachers could share whiteboard with students and can teach them.
## What it does
It is online whiteboard and graphic editor, one can share whiteboard to others and it can also used for web and graphic designing purposes .
## How we built it
We build it with frontend - html css ,javascript
backend - nodejs
API's - google font and bootstrap.
## Challenges we ran into
As I was new to google font api , was unable to merge with project.
## Accomplishments that we're proud of
To help students , teachers , graphic designers and many more in this covid time.
